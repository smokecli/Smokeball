## Introduction

Smokeball is an AI-powered legal practice management platform designed for high-throughput law firms that care about billing accuracy, matter visibility, and repeatable processes. Its matter-centric workspace consolidates intake data, contacts, tasks, calendaring, email, documents, and communications so every action is captured in context. Automatic time and activity tracking reduces lost billables by recording work as you draft, review, or correspond, including through Microsoft 365 integrations with Word and Outlook. Document automation and a large legal forms library help teams generate consistent, data-driven documents at scale.

For finance teams, Smokeball supports hourly, fixed-fee, contingency, and LEDES billing, plus batch billing and trust accounting tied directly to the matter ledger for cleaner reconciliation. The ecosystem extends via integrations for QuickBooks Online and LawPay, and optional workflows for DocuSign e-signatures and InfoTrack e-filing. Advanced reporting and firm insights help partners analyze utilization, realization, and profitability by lawyer, client, and practice area. With web-first and desktop workflows available, Smokeball can standardize operations across distributed teams while keeping work product centralized, searchable, and auditable on AWS-based cloud infrastructure.
